Weekly Reflection
How to return to clarity.
Most inner chaos does not begin with life itself.
It begins when the mind keeps returning to the same thought until that thought becomes the atmosphere of the day.
This week’s practice
Notice the first unnecessary thought and refuse to continue it.
Pause.
Breathe.
Return to what is real.
Return to what is in front of you.
Return to what is yours to govern.
This is how calm begins. Not with force. With awareness.
