Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

INNER VIRTUE X primarily offers digital products. Because digital resources are delivered instantly and cannot be returned in the same way as physical goods, all sales are generally considered final.

Digital Product Refunds

Refunds are not typically provided for completed purchases of digital products such as eBooks, guides, journals, or downloadable materials.

Exceptions

We may review refund requests in limited cases, including:

  • Duplicate charges for the same order.
  • Payment taken but product not delivered due to a technical issue.
  • Unauthorized transaction concerns reported promptly.

Support Path

Buyers should first use Xbot on the website for immediate guidance on delivery or payment issues. If the issue still requires manual review, the official support channel listed on the website may be used for follow-up.

Request Window

If you believe your case qualifies for review, contact us within 7 days of purchase with relevant order details.

Cancellations

Since digital products are delivered instantly, cancellations are generally not possible once a purchase has been completed.

Shipping Policy

INNER VIRTUE X currently sells digital products only. No physical shipping is involved.

Contact

For refund-related questions, use Xbot on the website or contact the official INNER VIRTUE X Instagram account at @innervirtuex.
